Undergraduate Program: Information Systems

The Bachelor of Science in Information Systems program prepares students for diverse careers in the IS field, including Systems Analysis, Computer Networking, and Health Care Informatics.

Curriculum

  • IS 300 – Management Information Systems
  • IS 310 – Software and Hardware Concepts
  • IS 410 – Introduction to Database Design

For a detailed list of courses, visit the official curriculum page.

Admission Requirements

Prospective students must meet general education requirements outlined in the UMBC Undergraduate Catalog. Specific prerequisites include:

  • MATH 155 – Applied Calculus
  • IS 147 – Introduction to Computer Programming

Career Opportunities

Graduates can pursue careers in areas such as:

  • Systems Analysis and Design
  • Computer Networking
  • Health Care Informatics
